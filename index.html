About The Firm

35 years of trusted legal expertise

Founded in 1987 by Patrick Murphy, Murphy & Sons has grown from a small Dublin practice into one of Ireland's most respected full service law firms. Our reputation is built on decades of delivering expert legal advice with honesty, clarity and a genuine commitment to our clients' best interests.

We believe that everyone deserves access to clear, straightforward legal advice. We take time to understand your situation, explain your options in plain language and guide you to the best possible outcome.
